Who are we?

Welcome to the Burnside High School Lion Dance Club! We are a vibrant and dynamic group dedicated to the traditional art of Chinese lion dancing, a cultural performance that brings joy, excitement, and good fortune to our community. Our club is open to all students, who share a passion for this captivating art form.

We have performed at various school events, such as the annual Burnside Cultural Day, and other occasions such as the 2025 China Study Tour. Performances are a big part of Lion Dance, and there will be countless opportunities to perform, such as festivals and ceremonies.

The Lion & Music

The lion is the heart of the performance, bold, colorful, and full of personality. Every movement tells a story, from playful gestures to powerful leaps, bringing good luck and driving away bad spirits.

The music gives the lion its life. Drums, cymbals, and gongs set the rhythm, guiding the dancers' movements and building the energy of the performance. The louder the beat, the more exciting.

The Spirit of Lion Dance

Whether you want to connect with culture, try something new, or simply be part of a supportive team, lion dance has a place for you. It's about energy, rhythm, and tradition, but also about friendship, confidence, and fun.
