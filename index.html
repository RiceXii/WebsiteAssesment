Who are we?

Welcome to the Burnside High School Lion Dance Club! We are a vibrant and dynamic group dedicated to the traditional art of Chinese lion dancing, a cultural performance that brings joy, excitement, and good fortune to our community. Our club is open to all students, who share a passion for this captivating art form.

We have performed at various school events, such as the annual Burnside Cultural Day, and other occasions such as the 2025 China Study Tour. Performances are a big part of Lion Dance, and there will be countless opportunities to perform, such as festivals and ceremonys.

Want to join?

We meet every Thursday at 2:45PM in G1.

Come along and give it a try!
